What is a Seahorse and How Many Until They Call it Smuggling

Seahorses are pimps. They live in water, breath in straight gas, slang rock and have a swim bladder. However they do not have caudal fins and have a long snake-like tail. They also have a neck and a shnozz that points down.

Eyesight

Seahorses have crazy eyesight, catch you on the wrong block 6 blocks down the line, and their eyes are able to work independently on either side of their head. This means they can look forwards AND backwards, can't sneak up on a seahorse. This is particularly useful as can see the punk bitch you are.

Shnozz

Seahorses have long thin shnozz enabling them to probe into the nooks and crannies of the finest babes on the block. When they find a seahorse babe they suck em up through their snouts like a vacuum cleaner. Their snouts can expand if the playa got a little room in there. They are not able to chew but they sure eat it.

Diet

Seahorses eat people all the time. WE MUST EAT THEM FIRST. Seahorse fry(delicious), eat a staggering 3000 pieces just for you .

Tail

Seahorses have a prehensile tail. This allows them to grip onto eelgrass and other weeds and prevents them from being washed away by strong currents and waves.

Color

Seahorses can change color very quickly and match any surroundings in which it finds itself. They have even been known to turn bright red to match floating debris.

Both males and females also change colour during their courtship display.

Body

Unlike most other fish, seahorses have an exoskeleton. Their bodies are made up of hard, external, bony plates that are fused together with a fleshy covering. They do not have scales.
